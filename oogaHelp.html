oogaSalad Game Documentation

This game engine allows the development of turn based strategy games on a grid playing field. Simply author the game setting, click play and you have made your very own game!

Authoring a game

Start off by naming your game, define your starting level, grid size and difficulty level

When the field is set, define your characters and objectives! To add a character, click on the characters on the right side of the GUI and then click the desired box on the grid.

Define the objective for the level by choosing them in the objectives box. If an additional level is desired, click the new level button and define your characters and objectives for the new level.

When you have authored to your hearts content, click play!

Playing the game

Click on an unit and their range of movement will be highlighted. Choose the action they should perform from the list on the right hand side and enjoy the game!

AI

This game can be made into a single player game by selecting the AI option and choosing how smart the computer AI is.

Tips on playing

  • Be sure to wait for the prompt to disappear before making any moves.
  • On any given turn, you are allowed to move and then perform any action. You may also perform an action without moving, if you never click move. Or you can move without acting, if you select "Wait" on your second move. You cannot move twice.
